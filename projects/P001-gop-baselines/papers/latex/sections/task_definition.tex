\section{Task Definition}

P001 studies \emph{transcript-known} pronunciation assessment. The system is
given learner audio together with the expected transcript. The transcript is
converted to a canonical phone sequence, and the scoring pipeline estimates how
well the observed speech realizes those expected phones.

This distinction matters. The task in this paper is not unscripted or
free-response CAPT. It assumes the target content is known, which removes ASR
hypothesis errors from the evaluation and lets the paper focus on the scoring
mechanism itself.

The final version should define the evaluation target precisely, including the
human phone-level and utterance-level supervision available in SpeechOcean762.
