\section{Discussion}

The discussion should answer three questions:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item how much do contextual scorers help relative to scalar scoring,
  \item how much does the backend choice matter under a fixed scorer family,
  \item whether mixed scalar variants provide meaningful gains over plain GOP-SF.
\end{enumerate}

It should also state the scope limits explicitly. This paper does not solve
unscripted CAPT, semantic coherence, or streaming inference. Those belong to
later project tracks.
