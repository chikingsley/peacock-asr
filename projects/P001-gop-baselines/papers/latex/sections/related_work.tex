\section{Related Work}

This section should cover three adjacent lines of work:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item classical and segmentation-free GOP for pronunciation assessment,
  \item contextual pronunciation scorers such as GOPT,
  \item phoneme-recognition backends used as upstream evidence sources.
\end{enumerate}

The final version should cite the canonical GOP-SF paper, GOPT, SpeechOcean762,
and the strongest recent pronunciation-assessment baselines that are actually
comparable to this setup. It should avoid drifting into unrelated ASR or
speech-LLM literature unless that work directly affects the scoring contract.
