\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

\looseness=-1
We present \ours{}, a multilingual model for simultaneous and expressive speech and text translation without requiring word-level alignment of translation data for training. Our method leverages coarse sentence-level alignments to train a base model that is further refined through Reinforcement Learning using process rewards based on BLEU score only. \ours{} outperforms the state-of-the-art across multiple languages with better quality/latency trade-offs, speaker identity transfer and speech naturalness. Moreover, we demonstrate new language adaptation with our method using less than 1000 hours of speech data. We release \ours{} weights as well as our multilingual long-form evaluation dataset to benefit the research community.
