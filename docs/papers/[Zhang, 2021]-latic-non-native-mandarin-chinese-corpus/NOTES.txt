Dataset Summary
---------------
- 4 non-native Mandarin Chinese speakers (L1: Russian, Korean, French, Arabic)
- 2 male, 2 female, ages 19-30
- 4 hours of speech, 2,579 audio samples (~9-10 s average)
- 1,423 unique pinyin units + 1 blank token
- 3 levels of annotation per waveform (original text, original notation, testing)
- Annotated by 2 Chinese-major students (native Mandarin speakers)

Files on IEEE DataPort
----------------------
- LATIC Speech Corpus.zip (391.76 MB) -- all audio WAV files
- Original Text Scripts (4 TXT files, one per speaker)
- Original Notations  (4 TXT files)
- Testing Notations   (4 TXT files)
- Testing Text Scripts (4 TXT files)
- Speaker.txt metadata

Significance
------------
LATIC is the only publicly available L2 Mandarin Chinese dataset for
MDD/pronunciation assessment training, making it a small but important
resource. It is cited in the Pitch-Aware RNN-T paper (Wang et al.,
Interspeech 2024) as the sole open L2 Mandarin corpus.
